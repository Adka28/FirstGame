Lets play!

Move the blue square through maze onto the red square and collect the gold. Beware of deadly brown traps. Keep healthy with green squares. Try pink surprise!

Use one or more of the following commands: u - Up, d - Down, r - Right, l - Left.
For example, command "ddru" will move two cells down, then one to the right and one up.
The best Gold Runner can collect all the gold!
